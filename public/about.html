About Me

Principal Data Scientist with 15+ years in AI/ML, telematics, and advanced analytics—turning high‑volume signal data into measurable business outcomes.

My Story

I specialize in building predictive models and analytics for IoT/Telematics—vehicle health, driver behaviour, EV range and battery health, and road surface classification. I love converting noisy signals into clear decisions that reduce cost, improve reliability, and enhance sustainability.

I’ve led data science initiatives at Danlaw (DeepView Analytics) and Darby Telematics (C6 Insights), collaborating closely with engineering and product. My work has enabled multi‑million dollar savings, ~30% data accuracy improvements, and proactive maintenance at fleet scale.

I enjoy mentoring teams, partnering with stakeholders, and delivering solutions that are explainable, dependable, and business‑friendly. I actively adopt GenAI (LLMs/RAG) into ML workflows—for faster feature ideation, documentation, and human‑readable explanations—while keeping model quality and governance front and center.
